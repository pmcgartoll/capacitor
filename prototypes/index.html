Deckbuilder prototypes

Four rough, self-contained visual prototypes — one per concept in game-ideas/. They aren't real games: they exist to give a quick feel for each core loop so you can sense which direction is fun.

Best viewed on a phone (or a narrow window). Tap a card to open its prototype.
